BHOPAL MUNICIPAL BLOODBANK

Donate blood, donate life!

The Bhopal Municipal Blood Bank was established and inaugrated by the then Hon'ble Chief Minister Mr.Shivraj Singh Chauhan. Since then it has been an integral part of the medical industry in Bhopal and its neighbouring cities and villages which also depeds on it. It is the largest blood bank of the central India region with cutting edge technology for storage,delivery and database of records since its inception.

The services are available to all affiliated and non affiliated hospitals with proper paperwork. Proper storage of all 8 blood groups and frequent blood donation camps that are organised by the management staff have kept the blood bank on its toes for every request that has been presented before the bank.

The present management is headed by Dr.Sanjeev Singh,Managing Director of the bank since 2019. The operations chief is Dr.Amit Kumar,since 2018. These gentleman and the able team of 50 nurses and wardboys are the reason behingd the proper working of the blood bank.
